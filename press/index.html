What is Heavy Metal Tennis Training?

Be an aging Rockstar. Take down an evil tennis machine outside your mansion while listening to 8Bit inspired heavy metal music.

Features

  • Simple but challenging Gameplay
  • 8Bit Graphics
  • 8Bit Heavy Metal Soundtrack
  • One-Touch / One Hand Gameplay
  • Game Center Leaderboard

About Shape Minds

Shape Minds is a Game Studio in Berlin, founded 2015 by Uwe Flade and Helen Choy. Uwe directed music videos for bands like Depeche Mode, Rammstein, Nickelback, Franz Ferdinand and many more. He produced million selling apps like Nighty Night! and Little Fox Musicbox. Helen Choy worked for first class motion design companies like psyop.tv, motion theory and buck NY.

About Snake Oil Studios

Snake Oil Studios is a brand new Games Development Studio in the heart of Berlin. It was founded by Johan Dettmar and Sven Schmidt in October 2015. The endless jumping arcade game Ducklings is the first game of Snake Oil Studios.
